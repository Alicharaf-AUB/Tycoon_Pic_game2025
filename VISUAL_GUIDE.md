## ğŸ¯ Design Patterns

### Cards
- **Standard:** White bg, 2px gold border, shadow on hover
- **Premium:** Gold gradient bg, enhanced border, always shadowed
- **Hover:** Lift effect (scale-105), gold border glow

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Gold gradient, white text, hover scale
- **Secondary:** White bg, gray border, gold border on hover
- **Danger:** Red bg, white text, standard hover

### Typography
- **Display:** Large (3xl-5xl), bold, gold gradient
- **Headings:** 2xl-xl, bold, dark gray
- **Body:** Base size, medium weight, gray-900
- **Labels:** Uppercase, bold, tracking-wide, smaller

### Spacing
- Cards: p-6 (24px padding)
- Grid gaps: gap-4 (16px)
- Sections: mb-6 (24px margin-bottom)
- Tight elements: gap-2 (8px)
